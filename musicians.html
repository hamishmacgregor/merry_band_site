Meet the Musicians

Professionals with the skill to read a room and the warmth to make it sing

Every musician we represent is personally vetted for both technical ability and professionalism. These are people you can trust to turn up on time, look the part and deliver something memorable.

[ Photo: Clara Ashton ]

Clara Ashton

Piano & Vocals

A graduate of the Royal Academy of Music, Clara brings an elegant touch to everything from Bach to Bossa Nova. Her relaxed stage presence and warm voice make her a favourite for cocktail hours and intimate receptions.

[ Photo: Hartwell String Quartet ]

Hartwell String Quartet

String Quartet

Four conservatoire-trained players who have performed together for over a decade. The Hartwell Quartet specialise in ceremony music and are equally comfortable with Mozart, film scores and contemporary arrangements.

[ Photo: James Okafor ]

James Okafor

Saxophone & Jazz Trio

James leads the Merry Band jazz trio and is also available as a solo saxophonist. With a background in Ronnie Scott's house band and festival appearances across Europe, he brings genuine jazz pedigree to every event.

[ Photo: Calloway Ceilidh Band ]

Calloway Ceilidh Band

Ceilidh & Folk Band

Five musicians with deep roots in Scottish and Irish traditional music. The Calloway Band guarantee a full dance floor — they're energetic, funny and brilliant at getting guests of all ages involved in the fun.

[ Photo: Sophie Reeve ]

Sophie Reeve

Harp

Sophie studied at the Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama and has performed as a principal harpist with several regional orchestras. She offers solo harp for ceremonies and drinks receptions, with a repertoire spanning classical, Celtic and popular music.

[ Photo: Marcus Webb ]

Marcus Webb

Acoustic Guitar & Vocals

Marcus is a versatile singer-songwriter who blends folk, soul and indie influences into a sound that feels both familiar and fresh. Perfect for garden parties, outdoor ceremonies and relaxed evening receptions.
