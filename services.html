Services & Repertoire

Live music for every part of your day, tailored to your taste

What We Offer

We provide live music for every stage of your event — from the moment guests arrive to the final dance of the evening. All packages are fully customisable.

Wedding Ceremony

Set the tone as guests take their seats, accompany the processional and recessional, and fill the signing of the register with something beautiful. We work closely with you and your celebrant or officiant to get the timing exactly right.

  • Pre-ceremony background music (typically 30 minutes)
  • Bridal processional
  • Signing music (one or two pieces)
  • Recessional

Drinks Reception & Canapés

The golden hour after the ceremony is a perfect moment for relaxed, elegant live music. Our soloists and small ensembles move easily between tables and mingle naturally with guests without overpowering conversation.

  • Solo piano, harp, violin or guitar
  • Jazz duo or trio
  • Acoustic pop and contemporary covers

Wedding Breakfast

Unobtrusive background music throughout the meal, pitched at exactly the right volume so guests can talk comfortably. We can time a first dance or special song to coincide with key moments such as speeches or the cutting of the cake.

Evening Reception & Dancing

When it's time to celebrate, our ceilidh band or jazz ensemble will keep the floor full. We perform two or three sets with short breaks and are happy to coordinate with a DJ for the rest of the night.

Corporate & Private Events

We also provide music for company celebrations, milestone birthdays, garden parties and other private gatherings. Contact us with your brief and we'll suggest the right act for the occasion.
